Rice Crispy Square

This Classic, delicious snack has been making memories for kids and parents alike for generations

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 package (10 oz., about 40) JET-PUFFED Marshmallows

OR

  • 5-1/2 cups JET-PUFFED Miniature Marshmallows
  • 6 cups Kellogg's Rice Krispies cereal

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan melt butte rover low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat
  2. Add KELLOGG's RICE KRISPIES cereal. Sir until well coated
  3. Using buttered spatula or wax paper evenly press mixture into 13 x 9 x 2-inch pan coated in cooking spray. Cool. Cut into 2-inch squares. Best if served the same day
